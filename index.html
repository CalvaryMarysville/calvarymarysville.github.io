      Welcome,
      Join us on Sundays at 10am for our weekly study. In Romans 9 through 11 we see that God's will prevails. We also learn that each person is responsible for their own choices. Come see how God's will and man's choice work perfectly to accomplish God's purposes!

      Wednesday nights at 7pm We meet every Wednesday night for worship and and Bible study. We are currently studying the book of Genesis. We hope to see you here!

      About Us

      Simply put, we are a group of people who love Jesus. We love to worship Him, study His word and love on His people. Everyone is welcome at here CCM, whether you are a long time believer or someone who is still searching. We invite you to visit us at one of our weekly services. We look forward to meeting you! If there is something you would like prayer for, please email the Pastor.
